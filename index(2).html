India is the seventh-largest country in the world and one of the most culturally diverse nations. From the Himalayas in the north to the Indian Ocean in the south, India offers an unforgettable travel journey.

Culture & Heritage

India is known for its rich traditions, festivals, classical dances, architecture, and ancient history dating back thousands of years.

Popular Festivals

  • Diwali – Festival of Lights
  • Holi – Festival of Colors
  • Durga Puja – Celebrating the Goddess
  • Eid – Festival of Harmony
  • Christmas – Joy & Celebration

Indian Food

Indian cuisine is famous for its flavors, spices, and regional variety:

  • North Indian: Butter Chicken, Naan
  • South Indian: Dosa, Idli, Sambar
  • Bengali: Rasgulla, Fish Curry
  • Gujarati: Dhokla, Thepla
  • Goan: Fish Curry, Bebinca
